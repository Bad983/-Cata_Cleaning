Along the way which beauty runs when it enters the mind to awaken Love, Lisetta goes gaily, as one who thinks she can take me. And when she reaches the foot of that tower which opens when the soul consents, suddenly a voice is heard: ‘Turn away, fair lady. Do not stay for here within reigns another lady who asked for the ruler's sceptre as soon as she arrived; and Love gave it her.’ Lisetta, seeing herself dismissed from Love's dwelling, turns back all painted with shame.
