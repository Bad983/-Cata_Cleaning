Thus Love said: “False Seeming, 

on this condition you will enter my court: 

you will assist all of our allies 

and put our enemies down.

And to begin this business, I confer on you 

the title of king of deceivers; 

I decided this some time ago in council, 

for I was well acquainted with your treacherous ways.

Now I want you to tell us in this hearing 

where you dwell 

in case we have to find you,

and tell us what you do and how you do it. 

Make sure we understand these things well, 

and if you don’t, you’re truly an evil cheat.”
